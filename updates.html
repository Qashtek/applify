REQUIREMENTS FOR STAGE 1

Eligibility

This stage is open to contestants who have successfully registered and have a valid contestant ID.

Stage Overview

In this stage, contestants will carry out two tasks:

  1. Writing Task
  2. Media Task

Writing task

  1. Open topic: In this stage, contestants can write on any topic of their choice.
  2. Word Count: Minimum of 300 words.
  3. Submission criteria:
    • Post on your Facebook account and tag or mention the official Mindportal Penstars' Club page.
    • Attach pen-up contest flyer to the post.
    • Include your contestant ID in the post.
    • Use these hashtags: #PenupWritingContest #MindPortalPenstarsClub

Judging criteria for Writing Task

Contestants will be judged based on the following:

  1. Creativity and originality of the content (10 points).
  2. Clarity and coherence of the writing. (10 points)
  3. Engagement with the audience. (15 points)
  4. Word Count (15 points)
  5. Contestant flyer and Contestant ID attachment (20 points)
  6. Use of the hashtags (10 points)

Total: 80 points

Judgment criteria for Media Tasks

Contestants are required to follow our official social media accounts (20 points)

  1. Facebook (5 points)
  2. Instagram (5 points)
  3. X (Twitter) (5 points)
  4. Youtube (5 points)

Contestants who don't hit a benchmark of 90 points will be disqualified. Goodluck!
